" ABOUT ME "











Hello World! My name is Elton and i'm a year 1 computing student at the Singapore Management University

I've started film photography as a hobby for about a year and a half and it turned out to be extremely gratifying. There is something unique about this delayed production of film compared to instant feedback in modern cameras which makes it even more worth the hassle

The above are simple projects which i saw as a good way to get down and dirty with web development and data analytics this winter! Rather than merely solving problems using Python as taught in y1s1 modules, i wanted to see if i could create something that was more of a visual product

Most of it is self taught from youtube, freeCodeCamp and some paid courses on Udemy and HackWagon Academy. I believe in learning and that it is a lifelong journey to continue upgrading oneself. Feel free to look through!
